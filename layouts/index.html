Benefits

SPEED

Accelerate your
Dev and Test Loop

  • Test your cloud apps from the first line of code and get immediate feedback of your local changes without having to wait on cloud resources.
  • Use generic, reusable local API emulators - instead of mocking out cloud dependencies manually for each project.
  • Foster digital transformation by giving developers back control over their environments with sandboxes for quick experimentation.

QUALITY

Improve your cloud application's quality through simplified testing

  • Test more frequently and easily debug your applications through reproducible test environments.
  • Enable a DevOps mindset throughout your organization by individual empowerment and fast collaborative debugging on a shared application state
  • Avoid resource conflicts in shared dev. environments - simply use an isolated instance when testing your IaC scripts before pushing them to production

COST

Save time and resources

  • Keep your cloud bill manageable and predictable, avoid unpleasant surprises due to an accumulation of idle cloud resources and a lack of resource cleanups in your development and testing environments.
  • Enhance developer experience and team productivity by removing barriers and giving each team member access to their local cloud sandbox environment.
  • Save developer’s time spent on waiting for unnecessarily long resource deployment cycles against the cloud and dev environment cleanups.
